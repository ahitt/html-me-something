My Favorite Pups

I love dogs! Below you'll find a list of the greatest dogs I've ever met!

"Sunshine"


I adopted Sunshine in Texas when she was 6 months old. She is the sweetest dog and loves people.
All she wants to do is go on walks, snuggle, and give kisses.

  • Age: Nine years old
  • Breed: Boxer/Shepard Mix
  • Favorite Activity: Long walks in the park

"Kaine"


My boyfriend and I adopted Kaine when he was just a few months old. Kaine is a high-energy pup
but gets along with all dogs and all people. All he wants to do is chew on toys and love others!!!

  • Age: 3 years old
  • Breed: Pitbull Mix
  • Favorite Activity: Going to the dog park to make new friends

"Lucy"


Lucy is a friend's dog who I dogsit for multiple times a year.
She is a sweet and slobbery young pup who loves to run!

  • Age: 2 years old
  • Breed: Poodle
  • Favorite Activity: Playing in the backyard

"Gus"


Gus is a sweet young pup who belongs to one of my old roommates.
Gus lived with us for a while where Sunshine and Kaine showed him the ropes.
He's a cute, snuggly pup who loves to explore!

  • Age: 1 year old
  • Breed: Corgi/Austrialian Shepard Mix
  • Favorite Activity: Peeing in the house

"Addie"


Addie is my brother's dog who I dogsit for when he goes out of town. Addie is a very laid-back dog
that just likes to lay around and bask in the sun!

  • Age: 2 years old
  • Breed: Lab Mix
  • Favorite Activity: Sun bathing

"Capone"


Capone is my friend, Jeff's dog. He is huge (almost 100 lbs.),
loves playing fetch, and pretending he's a small lap dog.

  • Age: 2 years old
  • Breed: Bullmastiff Mix
  • Favorite Activity: Slobbering
